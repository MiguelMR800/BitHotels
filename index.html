BitHotels - Crypto Payments Explained

Welcome to the BitHotels blog. Here you will learn how to pay for hotels using cryptocurrency.

Why Use Cryptocurrency for Hotel Bookings?

Cryptocurrency provides a fast, secure, and private way to book your hotel stays without needing a credit card.

How to Use Cryptocurrencies for Hotel Bookings

Follow these steps to pay for your hotel with crypto:

  1. Choose a hotel on BitHotels.
  2. Click "Book Now".
  3. Select "Pay with Crypto" at checkout (where available).
  4. Connect your crypto wallet and confirm the payment.

Supported Wallets

  • MetaMask - Easy to use and widely supported.
  • Trust Wallet - Multi-chain support.
  • Coinbase Wallet - Secure and beginner-friendly.
  • Ledger Wallet - Secure hardware wallet.

Supported Cryptocurrencies

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Tether (USDT)
  • USD Coin (USDC)
  • Binance Coin (BNB)

Supported Blockchains

  • Bitcoin Network (BTC)
  • Ethereum Network (ERC-20)
  • Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)
  • Polygon (MATIC)
  • TRON (TRC-20)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I use any cryptocurrency?

No, it depends on the hotel provider. Most accept BTC, ETH, and stablecoins.

What if my payment doesn't go through?

Contact the hotel's support. Always double-check wallet addresses.

How can I get the best price?

Compare hotel options on BitHotels and look for discounts.
