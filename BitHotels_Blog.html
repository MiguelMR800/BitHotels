Welcome to BitHotels, your guide to booking hotels using cryptocurrency. Learn how you can pay for your next stay with your favorite digital assets.

Supported Wallets

  • MetaMask
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet

Supported Cryptocurrencies

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Binance Coin (BNB)
  • USDT (Tether)
  • USD Coin (USDC)

How to Pay with Crypto

1. Choose your hotel from BitHotels.
2. Select your payment method as cryptocurrency.
3. Connect your crypto wallet and confirm the payment.
