BitHotels - Crypto-Friendly Hotels Blog

Cryptocurrency is revolutionizing the travel industry, offering a fast, secure, and borderless way to pay for your stays. At BitHotels, we aim to provide a seamless experience for those who prefer using digital assets. Whether you are an experienced crypto user or a newcomer, this guide will help you understand how to use crypto for your next booking.

Welcome to BitHotels, your guide to booking hotels using cryptocurrency. Discover the advantages of using digital assets for your travels and learn how to make secure and fast payments with crypto.

Supported Cryptocurrencies

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Binance Coin (BNB)
  • USDT (Tether)
  • USD Coin (USDC)

How to Pay with Crypto

1. Choose your hotel from BitHotels.
2. Select your payment method as cryptocurrency.
3. Connect your crypto wallet and confirm the payment.
