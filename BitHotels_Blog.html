Why Book Hotels with Crypto?

Traditional hotel payments often involve high foreign exchange fees, credit card processing costs, and regional restrictions. Crypto solves these problems by offering peer-to-peer settlement across borders — instantly and with minimal friction.

Top Benefits:

  • ✅ No currency conversion or banking delays
  • ✅ Global access, even without a bank account
  • ✅ Enhanced privacy and decentralized control
  • ✅ Potential discounts for using crypto at partner platforms

How It Works

Booking your stay with crypto is easier than ever:

  1. Select a hotel on BitHotels or supported partner sites
  2. Choose “Pay with Crypto” at checkout
  3. Connect your wallet (e.g. MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet)
  4. Confirm the transaction — and you’re booked

Supported Wallets

  • MetaMask
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet

Supported Cryptocurrencies

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Binance Coin (BNB)
  • Tether (USDT)
  • USD Coin (USDC)

Where Can You Book?

We feature affiliate listings from platforms like:

Anonymous or KYC-Free Booking?

Some platforms like LockTrip allow users to book without full KYC, offering a more private and censorship-resistant travel experience. Always verify the privacy policy of each hotel partner before booking.

What's Next for Crypto Travel?

The future of travel is decentralized. Smart contracts could eventually handle entire booking workflows, tokenized loyalty programs will replace outdated point systems, and NFTs may unlock exclusive travel experiences.
